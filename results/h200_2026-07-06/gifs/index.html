tinyvla — SmolVLA rollouts (Step B checkpoint, n_action_steps=10)

H200-trained checkpoint (frozen expert-only, 120 sim episodes, 6000 steps). On the training box (CUDA) this checkpoint scored 3/6 (50%) closed-loop. These clips are re-rendered locally on MPS, whose different numerics flip this marginal policy toward misses — so they under-represent it. What they honestly show: the arm reads the instruction and drives to the correct cube (min-dist ~0.03), then fails the precise grasp/place — the compounding-error story, visualized.
